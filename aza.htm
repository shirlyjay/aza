    How does "Save this Online ID" work?

     

    Saving your Online ID means you don't have to enter it every time you sign in.

     

    Don't save on a public computer

     

    Only save your Online ID on your personal computer or mobile device.

     

    How to clear a saved Online ID

     

    To clear a saved Online ID, sign in and select Saved Online IDs from Profile and Settings.
